About KVG Aviation Academy

Our Mission

At KVG Aviation Academy, our mission is to provide high-quality aviation training that meets industry standards, including specialized AME training and comprehensive CPL training. We strive to equip our students with the knowledge and skills necessary to succeed in their aviation careers, ensuring they are well-versed in aviation legislation and prepared for various pilot courses, as well as RTR training.

Grab interest

Discover the exciting opportunities at our training center, where we offer comprehensive AME training alongside in-depth CPL training and pilot courses. Our programs are designed to ensure you understand aviation legislation and receive the necessary RTR training to excel in your aviation career.
